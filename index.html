HOW DOES PRECOG FIT INTO MY APP?

Your existing database stores entity data, which change over time (people, places, things). Precog is designed for storing never-ending streams of event-oriented data, such as clicks, measurements, tweets, and transactions.

HOW DO I GET DATA INTO PRECOG?

Precog's simple REST API lets you capture events from any source, and on any platform (web, mobile, desktop, server). Precog scales automatically to capture and store all your data and ensures that no event slips through the cracks. You can focus on solving problems instead of maintaining infrastructure.

HOW DO I ENHANCE MY DATA?

Precog's marketplace lets you use third-party add-ons to add structure to unstructured data, clean up dirty data, normalize your data, and mashup your data with third-party sources. Apps in the marketplace let you import and export data from any source, visualize data, and much more.

HOW DO I ANALYZE MY DATA?

Precog's powerful REST API lets you perform deep analysis on your data, ranging from analytics, to statistics, to machine learning. You can focus on what you want to do with your data, instead of how to do it. With Precog, you never need to write any code to do any kind of data analysis.

BUILD IT YOURSELF VS PRECOG

Are you tired of writing thousands of lines of Python or Java to perform custom data analysis? Are you sick of worrying about how to scale up your infrastructure and keep it running 24x7 without losing even a single record? Do you wish you could focus on solving high-level problems in analytics, statistics, and machine learning, without being bogged down in low-level mechanical details? Then check out the Precog difference!

  • super simple

    There no software to download, no servers to setup, no configuration, no schema, and no limits. Just you, a code editor, and our super simple REST API. To make your life even easier, we also have client libraries for Ruby, PHP, Python, Java, C#, iOS, and more!

  • insanely powerful

    Our client libraries are jam packed with common operations like histograms, and time series, frequency distributions. When you need more, you can count on Precog's first-class support for Quirrel, the "R for big data" query language that lets you do arbitrary data analysis.

  • blazing fast

    Precog stores data in a columnar format, which allows Precog to compress data and only load relevant data during analysis. All of your data is stored in 100% Solid State Storage, with frequently used data promoted to memory, for the ultimate in performance!

  • rock solid

    Precog runs 24x7 with zero downtime, and never loses any data. The technology is backed by tens of thousands of automated tests, with continuous stress, performance, and regression testing. It's Enterprise-grade technology, without the Enterprise price tag!

  • infinitely scalable

    Precog lets you dump data in and run arbitrary analyses, without having to worry about scaling up the backend to keep up with ever-growing volumes of data. Focus on what you want to do with your data, not managing and scaling infrastructure!

  • rich ecosystem

    Precog isn't just an API — it's a rich ecosystem, teeming with both add-ons (which let you add structure to unstructured data, clean up dirty data, and mashup your data with third-party data) and apps (which let you pull in data, export data, and do BI on Precog).
