JOBS

  • VP of Business Development

    Boulder, CO, USA

    Precog is looking for a talented, ambitious, and data-driven individual to mastermind and execute on all revenue-generation opportunities derived from partnerships with other companies, with the end-goal of eventually fueling the majority of Precog's revenue through such partnerships.

    We're a small but fast-growing company vying to power the next-generation of intelligent, data-driven applications across web and mobile.

    Key Responsibilities

    • Revenue Generation Research, develop, and execute on strategies designed to maximize revenue from partnerships and distribution channels. Track and report on success of strategies and utilize data-driven processes to iteratively improve on strategy development and execution.
    • Partnership Development Identify, nurture, and secure strategic partnerships with companies in a variety of verticals which offer peripheral services of benefit to Precog customers (data services, data sources, business intelligence tools, etc.). Leverage partnerships to maximize exposure, develop brand recognition, and secure position of market-leadership. Utilize data-driven processes to quantify the benefit of partnerships at the individual and aggregate level, and focus on high-performing sectors.
    • Distribution Channels Identify, nurture, and secure new channels of distribution for Precog technology and marketplace solutions. Utilize data-driven processes to quantify ROI for every channel and focus on high-performing sectors.
    • Inside Sales Support Support developers as they evaluate Precog technology, helping developers make the business case for Precog technology to management, and influencing decision-makers at the management level. Develop and oversee processes to move inbound interest from management to technical evaluation. Work with marketing to develop separate messaging for management.

    About You

    You are independent, responsible, and self-motivated, and you thrive without a lot of structure or guidance. We want someone who takes initiative, self-optimizes, and is insanely productive.

    You can be trusted to do whatever it takes to get the job done, whether that's devising clever new ways to measure results, or working with legal to devise partnership agreements that afford greater benefits for Precog.

    You are passionate about Precog technology and enjoy working in the highly-technical space of "intelligence-as-a-service".

    You're data-driven, able to set concrete goals, devise actionable plans to achieve those goals, and then measure and report on results so you can iterate on the entire process.

    You are willing to travel from time to time. You should expect to be on the road about 6 - 12 times a year.

    Requirements

    This position is based in our Boulder, Colorado office.

    • BA or equivalent; 5 - 10 years of business development experience
    • Interest in technology, startup culture, and both business and customer development
    • Excellent oral and written communication skills
    • Ability to effectively negotiate complex business relationships
    • Excellent skills in business strategy development
    • Personable and outgoing, with a strong interest cultivating hundreds to thousands of professional and personal relationships to further Precog's agenda
    • Ability to collaborate and develop positive business relationships with all cross-functional areas within Precog and the Precog ecosystem
    • Smart-working and extremely productive
    • Excited about joining a small but growing team of 9 people
    • Willing to take on a wide variety of responsibilities
    • Exceptional organizational and project management skills

    Perks

    Precog is headquartered in beautiful Boulder, Colorado, home to a thriving tech scene that includes hundreds of startups and dozens of big name companies (Google, Microsoft, Oracle). Nestled in the foothills of the Rocky Mountains, Boulder has been described as America's best city to live in.

    Precog provides a comprehensive benefits package, flexible hours, and the chance to join an all-star team working on one of the biggest challenges of this generation.

"I've worked for excellent companies in the past, but with Precog I've found a place that is challenging, rewarding and fun."
